MEET OUR STUDENTS

Wongani Skosana is a competent young adult whose goal has been nothing but to aim for the stars. He always believed in self-motivation because growing up in a crowded location required him to trust himself more than he trusted others. Wongani was born in South Africa, Gauteng, and was raised in a small overcrowded township located north of Johannesburg called Diepsloot. His situation never stopped him from conquering it pushed him to work extra hard so he can escape his earlier life. Before Coding found him he had dreams of becoming a mechanical engineer, yet he's very grateful for falling in love with coding and making it his priority. With Coding he managed to find a better version of himself, spending most of his time thinking and talking about code made him realize how much he wanted to change the world with coding. He's elated for finding his calling in coding and plans on staying true to it. He has been a student of The Coding Ground for 13 full months, and he's thankful to The Coding Ground for helping him reach his full potential. The coding ground helped him acquire some skills which he needed in the Tech industry. Wongani Skosana believes in divide and conquer because he believes that working alone helps you think deeper within yourself and your working speed automatically rises.
