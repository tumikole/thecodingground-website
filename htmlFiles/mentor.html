MORAL JERA

STUDENT MENTOR

THE CODING GROUND

ABOUT ME

Moral Jera grew up in South Africa, Gauteng in a township called Olievenhoutbosch. He was raised by a single mother who made sure that she passed down a lot of valuable aspects of life to him. He was a little bit lucky because his mother managed to take him to a private school. After all, it introduced him to better education and better resources. He became fixated with computers in general, whilst doing grade 10 by that time he had no idea how computers operated, he just loved their physical appearance and went head over heels when he heard the sound his fingers made when dancing on the keyboard. He embarked on his software development journey in 2018 when he joined The Coding Ground. He is a devoted guy who tries by all means to perfect his work. He dedicated all of his energy and time to his Tech work. Because of consecrating his time he finished studying with The Coding Ground Earlier than expected and landed himself a job in a company called Devson. He also helps and assists students at The coding Ground in their growth and learning. His special fascination not only lies in uplifting himself but also in helping others understand how to solve problems that they encounter in their coding journey. As the saying goes when one hand washes another poverty could only be a story to be told, he did wash a lot of hands to help them reach their full potential. Dedication uplifted his life, so you can also do it.
